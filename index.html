Personal information

Currently I am a lecturer at Institute for Advanced Study in Mathematics at Harbin Institute of Technology. I completed my PhD thesis in Laboratoire de Mathématiques Blaise Pascal (LMBP) at Université Clermont Auvergne under the direction of Professors Arnaud Guillin and Liming Wu.

Research

My research interests are in kinetic theory and functional inequalities.
